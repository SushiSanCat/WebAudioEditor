About Web Audio Editor

A powerful, browser-based audio editing tool inspired by Audacity

Our Mission

Web Audio Editor was created with a simple mission: to make professional-quality audio editing accessible to everyone, directly in their browser. We believe that powerful creative tools should be available to all, without the barriers of cost, installation, or technical expertise.

By leveraging modern web technologies, we've built a tool that combines the power of desktop audio editors with the convenience and accessibility of web applications.
