Frequently Asked Questions

How do I record audio?

To record audio, click the "Record" button in the toolbar. Make sure your microphone is properly connected and you've granted the necessary permissions to the browser. Click "Stop" when you're finished recording.

How do I import audio files?

You can import audio files by clicking the "Import" button or dragging and dropping files directly onto the editor. Supported formats include MP3, WAV, OGG, and FLAC.

How do I apply effects to my audio?

Select the audio track you want to modify, then click on the "Effects" menu. Choose from a variety of effects like reverb, echo, compression, and more. Adjust the parameters to your liking and click "Apply".

How do I export my project?

To export your project, click the "Export" button in the toolbar. Choose your desired format (MP3, WAV, etc.) and quality settings, then click "Export". Your file will be downloaded to your computer.

Is my data saved automatically?

Yes, your project is automatically saved to your browser's local storage. However, we recommend exporting your work regularly as a backup. For the best experience, use a modern browser with good local storage support.
